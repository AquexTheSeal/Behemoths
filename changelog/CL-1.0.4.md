# BEHEMOTHS 1.0.4 CHANGELOG

--- 
### New Features
- Added the **Hollowborne** (and the **Hollowborne Turret**), a massive destructive sharpshooting Behemoth built to sustain, bruise, and pierce.
- Added 4 new Pandemonium Modifiers.
- - **Quickdraw**: Skeletons now gain increased movement speed and shoot more frequently.
- - **Heavy Arrow**: Skeleton now have a 15% chance to boost its arrows, gaining increased velocity and knocking targets back further.
- - **Overseer**: Hollowborne Turrets now gain a 30% damage reduction against any projectiles.
- - **Deadly Hollowcorper**: Hollowborne projectiles now gain increased velocity and damage.
- Added **Mortyx**, a new material used to upgrade Magnalyth weapons and tools for dealing with undead mobs.
- Added **Savage Flesh**, a flesh dropped by Archzombies and Banishing Stampedes.
- A bunch of minor QoL improvements, such as moving the Behebugger into the Operators Creative Tab.

---
### Bug / Incompatibility Fixes
- Fixed a client-side crash related to mount jumping by reworking the entire entity jumping system.
